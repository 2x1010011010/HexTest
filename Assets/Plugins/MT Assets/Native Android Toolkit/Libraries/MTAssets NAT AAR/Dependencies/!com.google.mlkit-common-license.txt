Privacy

When you use ML Kit APIs, processing of the input data (e.g. images, video, text) fully happens on-device,
and ML Kit does not send that data to Google servers. As a result, you can use our APIs for processing
data that should not leave the device.

The ML Kit APIs may contact Google servers from time to time in order to receive things like bug fixes,
updated models and hardware accelerator compatibility information. The ML Kit APIs also send metrics about
the performance and utilization of the APIs in your app to Google. Google uses this metrics data to measure
performance, debug, maintain and improve the APIs, and detect misuse or abuse, as further described in
our Privacy Policy (https://policies.google.com/privacy).
